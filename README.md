In this project, I served as a Data Analyst responsible for the end-to-end processing of a comprehensive customer dataset.
I performed critical data wrangling tasks, such as handling inconsistent values and encoding categorical features like gender and payment methods.
By generating detailed summary statistics, I was able to provide a clear overview of the dataset’s distribution and core characteristics.
Using Matplotlib and Seaborn, I created advanced visualizations to map out patterns in customer segmentation and internet usage.
My analysis revealed how specific demographics, such as age and location, directly correlate with different shopping preferences and habits.
I also focused on feature engineering to ensure that the data was perfectly formatted for deeper statistical exploration and reporting.
This work provided actionable insights that could help a business understand which customer segments are the most profitable.
Ultimately, I demonstrated the ability to turn raw, unorganized data into a structured narrative that explains modern consumer trends.
